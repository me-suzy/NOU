Database Administration/Add New Records To Database

To browse simply select the database you wish to browse (products, vendors, orders, or categories) and click execute without entering in any keywords.  To search enter up to seven (7) keywords seperated by commas or spaces.   Results are sorted chronologically (by date) in descending order.  You will be able to delete or modify information from the search or browsing results.  Adding new records/categories/etc will automatically update the html pages used for the mall or generate new ones.
